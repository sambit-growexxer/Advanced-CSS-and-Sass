About EcoTrips

We design sustainable trips that balance comfort, culture, and conservation. Every itinerary is crafted with local partners and small group sizes.

Our mission

Leave places better than we found them.

We carbon-offset 100% of trip emissions, support local conservation projects, and publish impact reports annually.

The team

Guides, fixers, and coordinators from the communities we visit. We prioritize fair wages and training.
