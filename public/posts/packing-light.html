How to Pack Lighter (and Smarter)

By Team EcoTrips • Updated Nov 2025

Less weight = more freedom. Here are our favorite pack-light tips from 1,200+ traveler departures.

1) Use the 1-2-3 rule

1 pair of shoes, 2 bottoms, 3 tops per week. Wash on the road.

2) Layers beat bulk

Thin, breathable layers adapt better across climates.

3) Refill, don’t carry

Collapsible bottles + local refill points cut plastic and weight.
